About 

Bamuda Corporation was established in 2012. It's a software consulting company focusing on Mobile applications development (both iOS (iPhone) and Android platforms), big data and cloud computing.

Bamuda has a team of experts in the above areas who are ready to offer excellent software solutions and services. Customers are our highest priority and we offer competitive price and top quality products.

Bamuda applies expertise in native iOS and Android development, social network conversation analysis, consumer behaviour analysis using big data technologies including machine learning,  data mining, statistics and MapReduce. We also develop backend software SDKs to support cloud computing using C++/C, NOTE.JS/Express.JS with MongoDb. We use an Agile management methodology to guarantee our products’ quality and delivered time.

Bamuda is committed to a culture of honesty, accountability and quality in all aspects.
